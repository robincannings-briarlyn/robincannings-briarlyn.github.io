About

Sector expertise. Strategic perspective.

Briarlyn Advisors was founded to provide the kind of rigorous, commercially grounded advice that energy companies and industrial organisations need as the sector undergoes structural transformation.

We work at the intersection of wholesale markets, regulation, and the energy transition - advising at director and board level on decisions where technical complexity meets commercial and policy risk.

Our clients include energy suppliers, generators, large industrial consumers, network businesses, and public sector bodies. We are equally at home advising on day-ahead market strategy and on ten-year decarbonisation plans.

Our approach

Precise

We say what we mean. Active voice, concrete figures, clear recommendations - not hedged generalities.

Forward-looking

Genuinely engaged
